What is Space Race?

Space Race is an educational website about space exploration, where you can learn about how space missions work in an easier and more interesting way. Space Race features a large collection of illustrative images created by me, the creator, to enhance your experience, as shown in the image where you can see the software I used whitch is Inkscape. It is recommended that as you navigate the website, you follow the sections in the order they appear on the homepage or the options menu in the upper right corner of your screen for a better experience. However, as a user, you are free to skip certain parts and go straight to the ones that interest you the most.

If you want, you can learn about the most important space missions that took place in each celestial body of the Solar System right here, where you can click on each button to expand the content about each planet.

You can also discover more about the biggest catastrophes in space exploration, such as Apollo 1, Soyuz 1, or the Challenger disaster right here.

To learn more about fundamental concepts of space exploration or even how technologies derived from it can benefit us today, just click right here.

To discover what the future of space exploration holds, what the main economic activities in space might be, and how the colonization of other planets like Mars could happen, just click right here.
